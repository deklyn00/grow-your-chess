How Does Chess Work?

Chess at it's core is a strategy game revolved around capturing an opponents king, winning either by checkmate, making the opponent surrender or just ending the game in a possible stalemate (a draw). See the linked video for a detailed explanation.

What is chess.com?

Apart from being the best platform to compete against other players in a game of chess online through ranked games, chess.com also offers many other useful features that can help you become a better player. Some features are set to a daily limit on the free version, however for those more serious about growing their chess... They may do the free 7 day premium trial and continue to use it if they like it or may cancel completely free of charge if within the 7 days.
